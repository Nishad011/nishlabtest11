Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior CAD experience to join?
No prior experience is needed for most courses. We start from absolute zero and build up to advanced level. Basic computer knowledge is sufficient.
Are the online classes live or recorded?
All classes are LIVE with a real trainer. Recordings are shared after every class so you can revise at any time.
What software do I need for online classes?
You need the specific CAD/CAE software (we help you set it up), Google Meet/Zoom for classes, and Telegram for support. All setup guidance is provided on enrollment.
Is the certificate from NISHLAB recognised?
NISHLAB CREATIONS is an MSME/Udyam registered institute. Our certificates are valid for job applications and demonstrate hands-on practical training to employers.
What is the fee structure?
Fees vary by course. Contact us via WhatsApp or the enquiry form for the latest pricing. We offer flexible payment options and occasional scholarships for meritorious students.
Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes! We help with resume building, LinkedIn profile optimisation, mock technical interviews, and direct referrals to our hiring company network.
Can working professionals join?
Absolutely. We have morning, evening and weekend batches designed for working professionals who want to upskill without disrupting their current job.
