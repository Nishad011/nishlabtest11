Who We Are

Engineering Excellence Since Day One

NISHLAB CREATIONS is a Mumbai-based engineering training institute founded with one mission: to give mechanical engineering students the real-world skills that colleges don't teach. We specialise in CAD, CAM, and CAE software training with a 100% practical, industry-first approach.

Our training methodology — what students lovingly call the "Wadala style" — is informal, Hindi-medium, and deeply practical. We break down complex software concepts into clear, actionable steps so every student, regardless of background, can master professional engineering tools.

From SolidWorks and CATIA to ANSYS and MasterCAM, our courses are designed to directly map to job requirements in manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and product design industries.
